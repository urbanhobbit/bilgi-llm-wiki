# ENCODE Project

**Full Title:** Unveiling Emotional Dimensions of Politics to Foster European Democracy
**Program:** Horizon Europe (Grant No. 101132698)
**Coordinator:** Re-Imagine Europa (RIE)

## About

ENCODE aims to decode the role of emotions in political discourse and their impact on democratic processes, creating positive narratives to foster trust in European democracy.

## Methodologies

- Social media sentiment analysis (6 European countries)
- Biometric research
- Surveys and citizen panels
- Citizen Innovation Labs (co-creation, WP6)

## 3 Best Practices (D3.4)

1. **Citizen-Centred Emotional Integration** — structured mechanisms for citizens' emotional perspectives in democratic processes
2. **Integrated Detection, Mitigation & Narrative Reframing** — combining early detection of emotional manipulation with platform governance
3. **Safeguards Against Negative Side-Effects** — preventing over-moderation, free speech tensions, and misuse of emotional analytics

## Policy Recommendations

- Emotional Impact Assessments (EIAs) for large digital platforms (DSA integration)
- "Slow Scrolling" behavioural norms
- Emotional literacy in media education
- Cross-sector research on emotion-centric harms
